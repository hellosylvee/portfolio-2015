About Sylvia

A native New Yorker, Sylvia is a product designer who is interested in the crossroads of design, technology, and education. Passionate about education and community, Sylvia is an organizer and volunteer at NYC UXPA and an associate board member at KAFSC.

On her spare time, she likes to work on projects, tinker with her raspberry pi, mess around with code and blog about it, and go on travels.
